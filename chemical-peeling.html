Reveal fresh, youthful, and radiant skin with our range of advanced chemical peels. Each peel is designed to target specific skin concerns – from dullness and acne to fine lines and pigmentation – leaving your skin smoother, clearer, and more refreshed.

GLYCOLIC ACID PEEL

Give your skin an instant refresh with our Glycolic Acid Peel – a gentle yet powerful exfoliating treatment that reveals brighter, smoother, and healthier-looking skin. Perfect for tackling dullness, uneven texture, and early signs of aging, this peel restores your natural glow in no time.

  • Removes Dead Skin Cells :- Unveils fresh, new skin underneath.
  • Brightens Dull & Uneven Tone :- Restores a natural radiance.
  • Clears Clogged Pores & Acne:- Improves clarity and prevents breakouts.

Reveal fresh, radiant skin with the power of Glycolic Acid Peel.✨

SALICYLIC ACID PEEL

Banish breakouts and regain clear, healthy skin with our Salicylic Acid Peel - specially designed to treat oily and acne-prone skin.

  • Deeply Cleans Clogged Pores :- Removes dirt, oil, and impurities.
  • Reduces Active Acne & Blackheads :- Clears breakouts and prevents new ones.
  • Controls Excess Oil Production :- Keeps skin balanced and shine-free.

Say hello to smoother, clearer skin - perfect for acne-prone and troubled skin types. ✨

TCA PEEL

Reveal smoother, brighter, and younger-looking skin with our TCA (Trichloroacetic Acid) Peel. This powerful peel penetrates deeper into the skin for intense exfoliation, stimulating cell renewal and collagen production for long-lasting rejuvenation. It’s the perfect solution for those seeking noticeable improvements in skin tone, texture, and overall radiance.

  • Improves Skin Texture & Tone :- Leaves skin smooth and refreshed.
  • Fades Dark Spots & Hyperpigmentation :- Evens out complexion.
  • Deep Rejuvenation :- Ideal for moderate to advanced skin concerns.

Transform your skin with the deep renewal power of TCA Peel.✨

AZELAIC PEEL

Azelaic peel is a gentle yet effective chemical peel ideal for sensitive and acne-prone skin. It targets pigmentation, inflammation, and bacteria without causing harsh irritation.

  • Brightens Dull Skin & Evens Skin Tone:-Restores a natural, radiant glow.
  • Controls Oil Production & Minimizes Pores :- Keeps skin smooth and balanced.
  • Gentle on Sensitive Skin:-Suitable for rosacea, redness, and irritation-prone skin.

Perfect for those looking for mild exfoliation with powerful skin benefits. ✨

LACTIC ACID PEEL

Lactic acid peel is a gentle yet effective chemical peel derived from milk. It's ideal for sensitive skin and beginners looking for smoother, brighter, and hydrated skin.

  • Deeply Hydrates & Improves Moisture:- Keeps skin plump and soft.
  • Refines Skin Texture & Smoothness:- Creates a silky, even finish.
  • Lightens Acne Scars & Sun Damage:- Promotes a clearer complexion.

Achieve hydrated, radiant, and youthful skin with the natural power of Lactic Acid Peel.✨
